Journal of the American Society of Echocardiography | 2024 | Lara-Breitinger KM, Miranda WR, Eleid MF, Smith TJ, Thomas JD, Nishimura RA

This study provides a critical evaluation of the accuracy of pulmonary artery systolic pressure measurements using Doppler echocardiography in patients with severe tricuspid regurgitation, offering mathematical modeling based insights into this challenging clinical population.
